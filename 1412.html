Platform 4.1: Live and Ready for Distribution

We’re pleased to announce that eXo Platform 4.1 is live! And while we’re planning a formal January launch, you can already test drive the new version. 4.1 is packed with updates like a simplified configuration system, email notifications, a manager add-on to quickly extend your Platform capabilities, and much more.

If you haven’t already, try eXo Platform 4.1 by registering to the community or downloading it now.

And, finally, a word of thanks for a great year—happy holidays to you all!
