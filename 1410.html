Q3: Posting Impressive Results

Things usually slow down in the summer. (It’s the heat, right?) Not at eXo. And we’ve got the numbers to prove it:

  • Double-digit growth for a fourth consecutive quarter (Q/Q)
  • Cross-product bookings were 30% above target
  • Triple-digit growth in new annual contract value (Y/Y)

Great, but what does it all mean? Some interesting trends bring it to life:

  • Growth in the Americas surpassed Europe for the first time in our history.
  • Negative churn! (Love that phrase.) Translation: the Community is growing!

Thanks to everyone in the Tribe for making this happen!
